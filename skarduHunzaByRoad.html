" 10 Days Tour to Skardu and Hunza By-Road"

Discover the unparalleled beauty of northern Pakistan with our Skardu and Hunza by Road Tour Package. Over several days, journey through breathtaking landscapes that include the majestic peaks of the Karakoram Range and the lush valleys of Hunza. Start with the serene vistas of Skardu, exploring landmarks like Shangrila Resort and Upper Kachura Lake. Traverse the scenic Deosai Plains, known for its high-altitude beauty, before moving on to the enchanting Hunza Valley. Experience the charm of historical sites such as Baltit Fort and the vibrant local culture. Enjoy comfortable accommodations, guided tours, and seamless travel logistics. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and cultural explorers, this package offers an immersive adventure through some of Pakistan’s most stunning landscapes.

Itinerary ( 10 days )

  • Day 1

    Islamabad to Chilas

    On Day 1, embark on an exhilarating drive from Islamabad to Chilas along the renowned Karakoram Highway. Enjoy picturesque stops along the way, including a visit to the stunning Naran Babusar Top, where panoramic mountain views await. After a day of scenic travel and exploration, unwind with an overnight stay in Chilas, a charming town nestled in the heart of the mountains.

  • Day 2

    Chilas to Hunza

    On Day 2, journey from Chilas to the enchanting Hunza Valley, a region renowned for its stunning landscapes. Stop at the Rakaposhi View Point for breathtaking views of the majestic Rakaposhi peak. Explore the historic Altit and Baltit Forts, which offer a glimpse into the region's rich cultural heritage. Stroll through the vibrant Karimabad Bazaar, where local crafts and traditional flavors abound. After a day of exploration and cultural immersion, enjoy a restful overnight stay in Hunza.

  • Day 3

    Hunza Valley

    On your full day in Hunza Valley, immerse yourself in the region's natural beauty and cultural richness. Begin with a visit to Attabad Lake, where turquoise waters and dramatic surroundings create a serene experience. Marvel at the iconic Passu Cones and cross the thrilling Hussaini Suspension Bridge for an adrenaline rush. Take a scenic drive to the Khunjerab Pass, the highest point on the Karakoram Highway, offering spectacular mountain vistas. Explore local villages like Hopper and Nagar to experience traditional life and stunning landscapes. Conclude your day with a comfortable overnight stay in Hunza, savoring the memories of a truly remarkable adventure.

  • Day 4

    Hunza to Skardu

    On Day 4, drive from Hunza to Skardu, taking in the scenic journey along the way. Make a memorable stop at the picturesque Rounu Valley, known for its serene beauty. Upon arrival in Skardu, spend the day exploring the charming Kachura area. Visit the enchanting Shangrila Resort, often called "Heaven on Earth," and enjoy the tranquil Upper Kachura Lake. After a day of exploration and relaxation amidst stunning landscapes, return to your accommodations in Skardu for a well-deserved overnight stay.

  • Day 5

    Skardu to shigar

    On Day 5, embark on a scenic journey from Skardu to Shigar. Discover the unique beauty of the Cold Desert, a stark and captivating landscape. Visit Blind Lake, a serene and hidden gem nestled in the region. Explore the historic Shigar Fort, which offers a glimpse into the area's rich cultural heritage. After a day of adventure and exploration, return to Skardu for a relaxing overnight stay, reflecting on the day's experiences amidst stunning surroundings.

  • Day 6

    Deosai

    On Day 6, embark on a full-day exploration of the majestic Deosai Plains. Begin with a visit to the picturesque Sadpara Lake for a brief yet refreshing stop. Continue to the Deosai Top, where you'll be treated to panoramic views of this high-altitude plateau. Discover the serene Bara Pani and the stunning Suarlake, both highlights of the Deosai’s rugged beauty. After a day filled with breathtaking landscapes and natural wonders, return to Skardu for a comfortable overnight stay.

  • Day 7

    skardu to Khpulo

    On Day 7, set out to explore the enchanting Manthokha Waterfall, a stunning cascade surrounded by lush greenery. Continue your journey to the serene village of Khpolo, where you’ll immerse yourself in the tranquil environment and local culture. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and natural beauty of this charming location before settling in for a restful overnight stay in Khpolo.

  • Day 8

    Explore khpolo valley

    On Day 8, explore the picturesque Khpolo Valley, discovering its scenic beauty and tranquil ambiance. Visit the historic Khapolo Fort, which offers panoramic views and a glimpse into the region’s rich heritage. Experience the local charm at the Thokhsi Khar Sailing Fish Farm, where you can appreciate traditional aquaculture practices. After a day of exploration and cultural immersion, return to Skardu for a comfortable overnight stay.

  • Day 9

    skardu

    On Day 9, explore Skardu's local attractions, starting with a visit to the vibrant market, where you can shop for unique souvenirs and local crafts. Next, discover the historical Kharpocho Fort, offering panoramic views of the surrounding area. Experience the charm of an organic village, showcasing sustainable practices and traditional life. In the afternoon, travel back to Chilas, where you’ll enjoy a relaxing overnight stay, reflecting on the day's discoveries amidst the stunning mountain scenery.
