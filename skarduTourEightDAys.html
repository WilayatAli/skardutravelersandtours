" 8-Day Skardu tour Explore Northern Pakistan"

Experience an 8-day adventure with our Skardu tour package, where you'll explore the breathtaking beauty of Pakistan's northern landscapes. Skardu, nestled in the heart of the Karakoram Range, offers stunning views of towering peaks, serene lakes, and vibrant valleys. Your journey begins with a scenic drive through rugged terrains, leading to the mesmerizing Skardu Valley. Visit the awe-inspiring Shangrila Resort, famously known as “Heaven on Earth,” and marvel at the turquoise waters of Sheosar Lake. Discover the historical richness of Skardu Fort and explore the unique culture of the region. Witness the enchanting Deosai National Park, renowned for its vast plateau and diverse wildlife. Enjoy the tranquility of the picturesque Upper Kachura Lake and the lush beauty of Lower Kachura Lake. With comfortable accommodations and guided tours, this package promises an unforgettable exploration of Skardu's natural splendor and cultural heritage.

Itinerary (8 days )

Below is the detailed itinerary for your Skardu Valley tour.

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Skardu

    On Day 1 of your Skardu adventure, you'll arrive in the enchanting town of Skardu and check into your hotel. After settling in, take the opportunity to wander through the vibrant local bazaars, where you can immerse yourself in the local culture and shop for unique souvenirs. Next, visit the historic Karapocho Fort, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes and an insight into the region’s rich history. Explore the charming Organic Village, known for its sustainable practices and local produce. Conclude your day with a relaxing overnight stay in Skardu, preparing for the exciting days ahead.

  • Day 2

    Skardu - Shangrila Resort and Upper

    On Day 2, after a hearty breakfast at the hotel, set out to explore some of Skardu's most stunning attractions. Begin with a visit to the Shangrila Resort, famously known as Lower Kachura Lake, renowned for its picturesque setting and serene environment. Next, head to Upper Kachura Lake, celebrated for its striking turquoise waters and majestic mountain backdrop. Spend time enjoying the tranquil beauty and lush surroundings of this captivating lake. After a day filled with natural wonders, return to Skardu for a restful overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Skardu - Shigar Valley and Shigar Fort

    On Day 3, start your day with breakfast before embarking on a scenic drive to Shigar Valley. Explore the unique Sarfaranga Cold Desert and the intriguing Blind Lake, adding a touch of adventure to your journey. Visit the historic Shigar Fort, a magnificent 17th-century fort-palace that offers a glimpse into the region’s rich heritage. Wander through the charming Shigar Valley, where traditional wooden homes and serene landscapes create a picturesque setting. After a day of exploration, return to Skardu for a comfortable overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Skardu - Khaplu Valley

    On Day 4, enjoy a delicious breakfast at the hotel before setting off for Khaplu Valley. This scenic drive takes you to a region renowned for its historic Khaplu Palace and tranquil landscapes. Explore the impressive Khaplu Palace, which offers a glimpse into the area’s royal past and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Visit the Chaqchan Mosque, an architectural gem with panoramic vistas of Masherbrum Peak in the distance. After a day of discovery, settle in for a peaceful overnight stay in Khaplu.

  • Day 5

    Khaplu - sailing Valley

    On Day 5, enjoy breakfast in Khaplu before embarking on a drive to the enchanting Sailing Valley. Once there, explore the unique formations and scenic beauty of the valley, known for its striking landscapes and distinct features. After taking in the sights, return to Skardu for the evening. Check in to your hotel and relax, preparing for another exciting day ahead.

  • Day 6

    Deosai National Park

    On Day 6, begin with breakfast at the hotel before heading out for a full-day excursion to Deosai National Park. En route, visit Satpara Lake, with its tranquil waters and stunning scenery, and marvel at the ancient Buddha Rock. Once you arrive in Deosai, immerse yourself in the park's expansive plains and search for its diverse wildlife, including the elusive Himalayan brown bears. Enjoy a scenic picnic lunch amidst the breathtaking landscapes. After a day of exploration and natural beauty, return to Skardu for a restful overnight stay.

  • Day 7

    Skardu to Basho Meadows

    On Day 7, start your day with breakfast before setting off for a full-day excursion to the picturesque Basho Meadows. This serene destination is known for its lush greenery, expansive meadows, and tranquil environment. Spend the day exploring the natural beauty and peaceful landscapes of Basho Meadows. After a day of relaxation and exploration, return to Skardu for an overnight stay, where you can unwind and reflect on the day's adventures.

  • Day 7

    Departure

    After enjoying breakfast at the hotel, you'll have some free time based on your departure schedule. Use this opportunity for last-minute shopping or to explore any remaining sights in Skardu. When it's time, transfer to Skardu Airport for your onward journey, concluding your memorable visit.
