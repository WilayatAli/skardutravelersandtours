" 5 Days by Air Skardu Tour Package Details"

Embark on an unforgettable 5-day adventure with our "5 Days by Air Skardu Tour Package," designed to showcase the breathtaking beauty of Skardu in the heart of the Himalayas. Enjoy the convenience of air travel, whisking you directly to this stunning destination. Your journey includes luxurious accommodations, guided tours of iconic sites like the serene Sheosar Lake, the majestic Deosai Plains, and the historic Skardu Fort. Experience the charm of local culture, relish authentic cuisine, and marvel at the awe-inspiring landscapes. With expert guides and seamless logistics, this package promises an effortless and immersive exploration of one of Pakistan's most enchanting regions. Perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike!

Itinerary ( 7 days )

  • Day 1

    SKARDU

    On Day 01 of your Skardu adventure, you’ll be greeted at Skardu Airport and whisked away to the picturesque Kachura Valley. Explore the stunning Shangrila Resort, often referred to as "Heaven on Earth," before taking a scenic visit to the tranquil Upper Kachura Lake. After a day filled with breathtaking views and serene landscapes, you’ll be transferred to your hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

  • Day 2

    Skardu – Deosai

    On Day 02, start your morning with an early breakfast before heading out to explore the stunning Sadpara Lake and the charming Sadpara Village. Continue your journey through Bara Pani to the breathtaking Deosai Plains, renowned for its expansive beauty and unique wildlife. Marvel at the serene Sheosar Lake, set against the backdrop of dramatic landscapes. After a day of awe-inspiring sights, you'll be transferred to your hotel for a restful overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Skardu- Shigar

    On Day 03, enjoy a hearty breakfast before setting out to explore the unique Sarfaranga Cold Desert. Experience the thrill of a quad bike and jeep safari across this arid wonderland. Visit the Shigar Viewpoint for panoramic views, and tour the historic Serena Shigar Fort, a testament to timeless architecture. Discover the tranquil Blind Lake, a hidden gem amidst the rugged terrain. After a day filled with adventure and cultural enrichment, return to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    (Skardu – Khaplu Valley)

    On Day 04, begin with an early breakfast before traveling to the majestic Serena Khaplu Fort, a marvel of historical architecture. Explore the nearby Chakchan Mosque and the serene Celling Fish Farm. Immerse yourself in local culture with a visit to the Polo Ground and the Old Mosque. Take in panoramic views from the Khaplu Viewpoint before heading to the tranquil Sogha Lake. After a day of exploration, return to Skardu for a restful overnight stay.

  • Day 5

    Skardu – Islamabad

    On the final day, enjoy an early breakfast before being transferred to Skardu Airport. Bid farewell to the majestic landscapes of Skardu as you prepare for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of this stunning Himalayan destination.
