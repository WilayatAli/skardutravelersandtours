" K2 Base Camp Trek (21-Days)"

The K2 Gondogoro La Trek is an exhilarating adventure that takes you through some of the most dramatic landscapes in the Karakoram Range. Starting in Skardu, this trek provides a unique opportunity to experience the awe-inspiring beauty of K2, the world’s second-highest peak, up close. The journey traverses the remote and rugged terrain of the Gondogoro La Pass, offering breathtaking views of K2, Broad Peak, and the Gasherbrum massif. The trek includes challenging high-altitude hiking, crossing glaciers, and navigating steep ascents and descents. Trekkers will pass through stunning valleys like Hushe and Sheosar, enjoying the diverse scenery and local culture. The high point of the trek, Gondogoro La, offers panoramic vistas that are both rewarding and unforgettable. The trek is physically demanding and requires good fitness, acclimatization, and preparation for altitude-related challenges. With a mix of adventure and natural beauty, the K2 Gondogoro La Trek promises an extraordinary experience in one of the world’s most spectacular mountain regions.

Highlights

  1. Gondogoro La Pass (5,585m). View four 8,000m peaks (K2, Gasherbrum I & II, Broad Peak) and other peaks (Gasherbrum 4, Laila, Gondogoro Peak etc) from one point.
  2. Base Camps of K2 and Broad Peak
  3. Night Stay at K2 base camp
  4. Circular/Circuit trek to scenic Hushe Valley
  5. Concordia – Throne Room of the Mountain Gods
  6. Trek along Vigne and Gondogoro glaciers from Concordia after trek to Godwin Austin glacier via Baltoro Glacier (5th longest and world’s largest outside polar region).
  7. View 8,000 m peaks including Gasherbrum 2 (8,035 m), Broad Peak (8,051 m) and K2 (8,611 m) from Goro II, Concordia & base camps.
  8. View of Glaciers (e.g. Biafo, Vigne, Liligo, Dunge, Muztagh, Biango, Yermanendu, Mandu and more) , glacial lakes, waterfalls and streams
  9. Meet mountaineers at base camps
  10. Sightseeing in Skardu and Islamabad
