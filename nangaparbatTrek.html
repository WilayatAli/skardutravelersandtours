" Nanga Parbat Base Camp Trek – 3,850 m (12,631 ft.)"

Embark on an exhilarating trek to the base camp of Nanga Parbat, one of the world's most formidable peaks, on a guided adventure through Pakistan’s western Himalayas. Beginning from Gilgit, either by air or road, this trek offers awe-inspiring views of Nanga Parbat and other majestic peaks as you traverse the scenic Rupal and Rakhiot valleys. Led by expert local guides, you'll experience the raw beauty, rich culture, and history of the region. Spanning 10-13 days, the trek's duration can be adjusted for weather and participant fitness levels. This demanding journey requires good physical condition and proper gear. Book your Nanga Parbat trek now to uncover the stunning landscapes and hidden wonders of Pakistan’s western Himalayas.

Highlights

  1. Drive through the Karakoram Highway
  2. Night stays at Fairy Meadows
  3. Beautiful views of Passu Cones, Hussaini Suspension Bridge and many more
  4. Circular/Circuit trek to scenic Hushe Valley
  5. Concordia – Throne Room of the Mountain Gods
  6. Explore traditional villages and learn about local culture and way of life
  7. See glaciers, lakes and rivers along the way
  8. Led by experienced local guides for an authentic experience.
