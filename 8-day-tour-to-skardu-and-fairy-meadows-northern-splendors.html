8-Day Tour to Skardu and Fairy Meadows- Northern Splendors

  • DESCRIPTION

Embark on a mesmerizing 8-day journey through the heart of Pakistan, exploring the enchanting Fairy Meadows and the breathtaking landscapes of Skardu. This carefully crafted tour package offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure. From the iconic Nanga Parbat to the serene Shangrila Resort, immerse yourself in the grandeur of Northern Pakistan.

Tour Highlights:

  1. Islamabad to Chilas: Begin your journey on the iconic Karakoram Highway, passing through picturesque landscapes and enjoying the scenic beauty of Abbottabad.
  2. Chilas to Raikot Bridge: Traverse through the mesmerizing terrain, reaching the gateway to Fairy Meadows at Raikot Bridge.
  3. Tatu Village Jeep Ride: Experience the thrill of a local jeep ride from Raikot Bridge to Tatu Village, the starting point of your trek to Fairy Meadows.
  4. Fairy Meadows Trek: Embark on a trek through lush landscapes, surrounded by the grandeur of Nanga Parbat, reaching the serene Fairy Meadows campsite.
  5. Nanga Parbat Base Camp (Optional): Hike to the base camp for an up-close encounter with the world's ninth-highest peak, Nanga Parbat.
  6. Fairy Meadows to Raikot Bridge: Descend from Fairy Meadows, returning to Raikot Bridge, and continue your journey towards Skardu.
  7. Three mountain Junction: Stop at Three Mountain Junction Karakoram-Himalaya-Hindukush.
  8. Skardu Valley Arrival: Arrive in Skardu, situated on the banks of the Indus River, offering a captivating view of the surrounding mountains.
  9. Shigar Fort: Explore the rich history of Shigar Fort.
  10. Shangrila Resort: Experience tranquility at Shangrila Resort while enjoying a boat ride on Shangrila Lake.
  11. Satpara Lake Excursion: Discover the serene beauty of Satpara Lake, surrounded by the majestic mountains of Skardu.
  12. Upper Kachura Lake: Visit the enchanting Upper Kachura Lake, known for its turquoise waters, and indulge in the natural splendor.
  13. Skardu Bazaar: Immerse yourself in the local culture and flavors of Skardu at the bustling Skardu Bazaar.
  14. Deosai National Park (Optional): Explore the unique landscapes of Deosai National Park, home to diverse wildlife and vast plateaus.
  15. Khaplu Fort: Explore the centuries-old Khaplu Fort and experience the old architectural building.
  16. Sarfranga Cold Desert: Visit the world's second-highest cold desert on the middle snow-capped mountain, on the Bay of Indus River.
  17. Monthokha Waterfall: Experience the down-falling crystal water on a rocky cliff, making the environment pleasant and adorable.

Day-to-Day Itinerary:

Depart from Islamabad early morning. Drive through the picturesque Karakoram Highway (KKH), enjoy the scenic beauty of Abbottabad, and stop for lunch in Besham.

Arrive in Chilas, check-in at the hotel, and enjoy dinner with a view of the surrounding mountains.

Breakfast at the hotel. Continue the journey towards Raikot Bridge, transfer to local jeeps for a thrilling ride to Tatu Village, and trek to Fairy Meadows.

Arrive at Fairy Meadows, set up camp, and witness the majestic Nanga Parbat. Dinner amidst the magical surroundings.

Full day to explore Fairy Meadows, optional hike to Nanga Parbat Base Camp, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of the meadows.

Capture stunning photographs and interact with the locals. Overnight stay at the campsite.

Breakfast at the campsite. Trek back to Tatu Village, drive to Skardu via Raikot Bridge, and marvel at the Rakaposhi viewpoint.

Arrive in Skardu, check-in at the hotel, and enjoy a relaxing evening by the Indus River. Dinner with a view of the Skardu Valley.

Journey to the picturesque Khaplu Valley. Visit the historic Khaplu Fort (500 years old) and immerse yourself in the local culture. Visit Chaqchan Mosque Dating from 1370.

Explore the awe-inspiring Manthokha Waterfall, a hidden gem in the heart of Skardu. Arrive in Skardu in the evening. Overnight stay in the hotel.

Start your journey from Skardu in the morning. Arrive at the historic Shigar Fort, a testament to the rich cultural heritage of the area. Visit Blind lake Shigar.

Toward Sarfaranga Cold Desert. Enjoy paragliding at Sarfaranga Cold Desert. Back to Skardu. Dinner at the hotel.

Depart from Skardu in the morning. The most common mode of transportation is by road.

The journey takes you through the Karakoram Highway (KKH), which offers breathtaking views but can be challenging due to its winding roads. The distance between Skardu and Chilas is around 330 kilometers. Overnight stay in Chilas.

Departure for Islamabad after breakfast. The most common mode of transportation is by road.

The distance between Chilas and Islamabad is approximately 280 kilometers. The journey usually takes around 8 to 10 hours by road, but this can vary based on road conditions and traffic. Arrive in Islamabad by evening.

Package Inclusions:

  1. Accommodation (3-star hotels in Chilas and Skardu, camping in Fairy Meadows).
  2. Meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner).
  3. Transportation (air-conditioned vehicle from Islamabad to Chilas and back).
  4. Jeep transfer to Tatu Village and local transportation in Skardu.
  5. Guide and porter services during the trek.
  6. All entrance fees and permits.

Package Exclusions:

  1. Personal expenses (phone calls, laundry, souvenirs).
  2. Extra meals or snacks not mentioned in the itinerary.
  3. Tips and gratuities.
